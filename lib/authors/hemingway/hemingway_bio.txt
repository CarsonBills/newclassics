Author Biography
<br>
Ernest Hemingway was an American writer born July 21, 1899 and died July 2, 1961. He published seven novels during his life and many short stories during his lifetime. He was a part of the "Lost Generation" of American expatriates, and much of his writing responded to the larger Jazz Age culture by emphasizing feelings of loneliness and an appeal to a connection with nature.
<br>
<br>
Hemingway wrote in a distinctly delicate language.  relying heavily on dialogue and simple language. He required a reader to parse out much of the understated meaning of his work on their own, rather than weaving sentimental sentences to communicate the emotions of the story.
